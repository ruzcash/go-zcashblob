## 0.1.0 - 2026-07-18

### Added

- Parsing and serialization of Zcash version 5 transaction blobs.
- ZIP-244 transaction ID and authorizing-data digest computation.
- Transparent, Sapling, and Orchard field support.
- Canonical CompactSize handling and defensive allocation limits.
- Official transaction-vector coverage, fuzz targets, and cross-platform CI.
- Public construction, structural validation, and conventional transaction ID
  display helpers.
